Our client

We built the station with an elementary school student, her teacher, and CIL Western Wisconsin. She told us what she needed. We designed to that, tested against it, and iterated. The product exists because she asked for independence, not assistance.

The course

GNG2101 pairs student teams with real clients who need assistive devices. Teams take a product from need statement to working prototype in one semester. This site documents our deliverable.
